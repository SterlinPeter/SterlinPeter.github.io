About Us

Our History

Started in 2015, Chocolate House quickly established itself as an icon par excellence in Pune. With its unique collection of creamiest cakes that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Pune.

The bakery traces its humble beginnings to The Cake Empire, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Abhishek Gupta, that featured for the first time the world's best cakes.

Corporate Leadership

Abhishek Gupta Chief Epicurious Officer

Our CEO, Abhishek, credits his hardworking East Asian immigrant parents who undertook the arduous journey to the shores of America with the intention of giving their children the best future. His mother's wizardy in the kitchen whipping up the tastiest cakes with whatever is available inexpensively at the supermarket, was his first inspiration to create the fusion cakes for which The Cake Empire became well known. He brings his zeal for fusion cakes to this bakery, pioneering cross-cultural baking connections.

Dhanasekaran Chief Food Officer

Our CFO, Danny, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in baking. His experiences growing up on a farm in the Australian outback gave him great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Sahil Patil Chief Taste Officer

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, Agumbe, our CTO, personally ensures that every cake that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their cake does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

Robert Ross Executive Chef

Award winning chef with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the bakery world, he specializes in creating mouthwatering Indo-Italian fusion experiences. He says, Put together the cuisines from the two craziest cultures, and you get a winning hit! Amma Mia!
